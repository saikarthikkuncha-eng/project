Frequently Asked Questions

How long does international shipping take?

Depending on the destination and the selected freight method (Air vs. Ocean), international shipping can take anywhere from 2 to 14 business days. Air freight is our fastest option.

What items are restricted from shipping?

We cannot ship hazardous materials, flammable liquids, firearms, or illegal substances. Please refer to our full Restricted Items Policy on your user dashboard for a detailed list.

How do I track my cargo?

You can enter your 12-digit tracking number on the homepage tracking bar or log into your SwiftMove account to view real-time GPS locations of your freight.

Do you offer cargo insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance for all shipments. You can opt-in for coverage during the quote request process to ensure your high-value items are fully protected.
